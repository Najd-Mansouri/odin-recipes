Hamburger

Description

A hamburger is a popular sandwich consisting of a cooked ground meat patty, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bun. Typically garnished with toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, and various condiments, hamburgers are a versatile and widely enjoyed meal, often served with sides like fries or salad.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (80/20 blend)
  • Hamburger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato, sliced
  • Onion, sliced

Steps

  1. Form the Patties
  2. Cook the Patties
  3. Toast the Buns
  4. Assemble the Hamburger
  5. Serve
