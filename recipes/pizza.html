Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a round, flat base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. Baked until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly, pizza is a versatile meal enjoyed worldwide, often customized to suit individual tastes.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • GTomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Olive oil
  • Toppings (e.g., pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Preheat the Oven
  3. Roll Out the Dough
  4. Add Toppings
  5. Bake the Pizza
